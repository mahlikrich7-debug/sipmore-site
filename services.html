Philosophy
How We Price

Scope first.
Price second.

Every venue is different. Rather than rigid packages, Sip More builds pricing around what your engagement actually requires — so you're never paying for things you don't need.

Starting rates are anchors, not ceilings. Final proposals are shaped by:

  • Venue size & complexity
  • Volume of monthly programming
  • Number of staff to train
  • On-site time required
  • Engagement duration
  • Travel & logistics
