Our Philosophy

We don't pour wine.
We build culture
around it.

Sip More was built on the belief that wine doesn't belong locked behind velvet ropes or intimidating lists. It belongs in the room — in the music, the conversation, the moment.

We work with hospitality groups who want their beverage programs to feel as intentional as the rest of the experience — and who understand that the right wine director changes the entire room.

Mahlik
Richardson

WSET Level 3 certified sommelier, beverage curator, and the founder behind Sip More LLC. Mahlik brings together a background in Four Seasons hospitality, deep roots in New Orleans culture, and a genuine obsession with how wine moves through a room.

His approach is simple: wine should live where people live. That means R&B nights, jazz clubs, hotel lobbies, and cookouts — not just tasting rooms.
